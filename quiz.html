Question 4 answers

a.

  • L1 hash = 01
  • L2 hash = 10
  • L1 || L2 = 0110 = 10

b.

  • L3 hash = 00
  • L4 hash = 10
  • L3 || L4 = 0010 = 01

c.

  • (L1 || L2) || (L3 || L4) = 1001 = 00
